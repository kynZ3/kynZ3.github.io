Quick Background

Christian Dixon, also known as “Cheetah,” has been an athlete since his early years. He began playing football in 5th grade and discovered his passion for track in 2016 when he joined the Charlotte Heat Track Club. Since then, he has excelled in both sports, earning numerous accolades and setting his sights on even greater achievements.

Current Accomplishments

  • Ranked #9 in the U.S. for the Class of 2025 in track
  • 3x State Champion in track
  • Recipient of the Charlotte Track and Field Award (Sprinter of the Year)
  • Turf Kings Southeast Invitational MVP
